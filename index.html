Numbers Game Solver

Sat, Feb 6, 2021 The Numbers Solver finds solutions for the “Numbers” game (as played on long-running game shows in the UK, France and Australia). Note that it doesn’t let you play the numbers game, it just finds the best solutions. Brief rules for the Numbers game: Six “source” numbers are chosen from a pool of twenty-four. The pool contains two each of all the integers from 1 to 10, plus one each of 25, 50, 75 and 100.
